FACILITIES

Hostel

The Institute houses the hostel providing comfortable stay facilities to 240 students in 80 rooms.

Canteen

The college has a lively canteen in which hot as well as cold beverages and packed, hygienic food is served.

Parking for students

The College has a separate parking lot in the campus for students.

Library

Well-stocked and presently having a collection of over three thousand volumes on different subjects.

Discipline

Campus is a non-alcoholic, no-smoking zone. Ragging is prohibited and is a serious offense

Sports

Ground-volleyball, football, basketball, badminton, lawn tennis, Kho Kho,

Student Financial Support

Institute is offereing Merit-based Scholarships for students, Scholarship for class toppers, Pragati Scholarship Scheme,

Transportation

Govt. Bus facility from Palwal to Faridabad

Training and Placements

Internships and placements in renowned Architecural firms

Extra Curricular Activities

Monthly cultural activities such as art & crafts, personality development, Yoga etc.

Curriculum

Totally digital teaching-learning environment, experienced faculty

Multipurpose hall

The hall has a seating capacity of about three hundred people. Extensively used for extra-curricular events and for hosting functions of the College such as the annual day,, also used for examination purpose.

Seminar Room

The air-conditioned seminar room, with a seating capacity of about sixty people, is equipped with audio-visual systems,a projector screen, white board, black board and a podium
